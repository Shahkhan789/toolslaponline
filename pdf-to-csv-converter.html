Frequently Asked Questions

Can it extract tables from scanned PDFs?

Our tool works best with text-based PDFs. Scanned or image-based PDFs may not yield accurate results as they require OCR processing.

What types of tables can be extracted?

We can extract most table formats including bordered tables, aligned columns, and structured data layouts with consistent formatting.

Is there a file size limit?

You can convert PDF files up to 50MB in size. Larger files with more tables may take longer to process.

How accurate is the table extraction?

Accuracy depends on the table structure in the PDF. Well-formatted tables typically achieve 90%+ accuracy in extraction.
