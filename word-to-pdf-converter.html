Frequently Asked Questions

How to convert Word to PDF for free?
Simply upload your Word document (DOC or DOCX) using the upload button above, choose your PDF format preference, and click convert. The tool will process your file locally and provide a download link for the converted PDF.
Is Word to PDF conversion safe and secure?
Yes, absolutely! Our Word to PDF converter processes all files locally in your browser using client-side technology. Your documents are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security for sensitive documents.
What Word formats are supported?
We support both DOC (Microsoft Word 97-2003) and DOCX (Microsoft Word 2007+) formats. You can convert documents created in Microsoft Word, Google Docs (when saved as DOCX), and other compatible word processors.
Will the formatting be preserved in the PDF?
Yes! Our converter preserves all formatting including fonts, colors, images, tables, headers, footers, page numbers, and layout structure. The PDF will look identical to your original Word document.
What's the maximum file size I can convert?
You can convert Word documents up to 50MB in size. This is sufficient for most documents including those with many images. For larger files, consider compressing images in your Word document before conversion.
