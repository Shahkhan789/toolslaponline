Frequently Asked Questions

How to convert PDF to Word for free?
Simply upload your PDF file using the upload button above, choose your preferred Word format (DOC or DOCX), and click convert. Our tool will process your file locally and provide a download link for the converted Word document.
Is PDF to Word conversion safe and secure?
Yes, absolutely! Our PDF to Word converter processes all files locally in your browser using client-side technology. Your files are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security for sensitive documents.
What's the maximum PDF file size I can convert?
You can convert PDF files up to 50MB in size. For larger files, we recommend compressing your PDF first using our PDF compressor tool, or splitting large PDFs into smaller sections.
Does it work with scanned PDFs and image-based documents?
Yes! Our converter includes advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology that can extract text from scanned PDFs, image-based documents, and PDFs containing photographs of text, making them fully editable in Word.
Will the formatting be preserved in the Word document?
Our converter uses advanced algorithms to preserve fonts, images, tables, columns, and overall layout structure. While most formatting is maintained perfectly, complex layouts may require minor adjustments in Word.
What's the difference between DOC and DOCX formats?
DOCX is the modern Word format (2007+) that offers better compression, security, and compatibility with recent versions of Microsoft Word. DOC is the classic format compatible with older Word versions. We recommend DOCX for most users.
