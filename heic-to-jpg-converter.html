Frequently Asked Questions

What is HEIC and why do iPhones use it?
HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is Apple's modern image format that provides better compression than JPEG while maintaining higher quality. iPhones use HEIC to save storage space while keeping photo quality high. However, many devices and platforms don't support HEIC, which is why conversion to JPG is often necessary.
How to convert HEIC to JPG for free?
Simply upload your HEIC files using the upload button above, choose your preferred quality setting, and click convert. Our tool will process your files locally and provide download links for the converted JPG images.
Can I convert multiple HEIC files at once?
Yes! Our converter supports batch processing. You can select multiple HEIC files (up to 50 files at once) and convert them all simultaneously. This is perfect for converting entire photo albums from your iPhone.
Will the quality be preserved after conversion?
Yes! You can choose from three quality levels: Standard (85%), High (95%), or Maximum (100%). The Maximum quality setting preserves the original image quality as closely as possible while converting to JPG format.
Is it safe to upload my iPhone photos?
Absolutely! All conversions happen locally in your browser using client-side technology. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security for your personal images.
What devices can open JPG files?
JPG is the most universally supported image format. It works on all devices including Windows PCs, Android phones, older iPhones, web browsers, social media platforms, email clients, and virtually any device or software that handles images.
