🔍 Luhn Algorithm

The Luhn algorithm validates credit card numbers using a checksum formula. It catches 100% of single-digit errors and 89% of transposition errors.

🏷️ Brand Detection

Card brands are identified by their Issuer Identification Number (IIN) patterns. Each brand has specific number ranges and lengths.

🔒 Security

This tool performs validation only and never stores or transmits data. Always use test numbers for development and never real credit card information.
