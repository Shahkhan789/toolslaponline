Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2025

🔒 Your Privacy is Our Priority - We Don't Track, Store, or Sell Your Data

Introduction

At ToolSlap, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our online tools.

The short version: We designed ToolSlap to be completely privacy-focused. Most of our tools process your data entirely in your browser without sending anything to our servers. We don't track you, we don't sell your data, and we collect minimal information necessary only to provide our services.

Information We Collect

Information We DON'T Collect

Personal identification information (names, addresses, phone numbers)
Files or content you process using our tools
Detailed browsing behavior or tracking across websites
Location data
Financial information
Social media profiles or connections

Information We May Collect

Basic Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as page views, tool usage frequency, and general geographic regions (country level only) to help us improve our services.
Technical Information: Basic technical information such as browser type, device type, and screen resolution to optimize our tools for different devices.
Contact Information: If you choose to contact us, we'll collect the information you provide (such as your email address and message content) solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

Providing and maintaining our services
Improving our tools and user experience
Responding to your comments, questions, and customer service requests
Monitoring and analyzing usage trends to improve our services
Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues

Local Processing

Most importantly: The majority of our tools process your data entirely within your browser using JavaScript. This means:

Your files and content never leave your device
We cannot see what you're processing
Your data remains completely private and secure
Processing happens instantly without internet uploads

Tools that work entirely in your browser include: Image Converter, Image Compressor, Image Crop, Password Generator, QR Code Generator, Text Formatter, Color Picker, Unit Converter, Hash Generator, Base64 Encoder, JSON Formatter, Word Counter, CSS Gradient Generator, QR Code Scanner, Markdown Converter, Lorem Ipsum Generator, and many others.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We may only disclose information in the following limited circumstances:

Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
Service Providers: To trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website (such as hosting services), provided they agree to keep information confidential
Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

HTTPS encryption for all connections to our website
Regular security updates and monitoring
Limited access to any data we do collect
Secure hosting infrastructure

However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use minimal cookies and similar tracking technologies:

Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
Analytics Cookies: Anonymous usage statistics (you can disable these in your browser)
No Advertising Cookies: We don't use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality but will not prevent you from using our tools.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
Objection: Object to the processing of your personal information

Given our privacy-focused approach and minimal data collection, exercising most of these rights may not be necessary, but we're committed to honoring them if applicable.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

International Data Transfers

Our website is hosted and operated in the United States. If you are accessing our services from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located and our central database is operated.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Significant changes to this policy will be prominently announced on our website.
