How to Play

Master the classic strategy game

Rules

1

Players take turns placing their marks (X or O) on a 3×3 grid.

2

The first player to get 3 marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.

3

If all 9 squares are filled and no player has 3 in a row, the game is a tie.

Strategy Tips

Center control: The center square is the most valuable position.

Block opponents: Always block your opponent's potential winning moves.

Create forks: Set up multiple winning opportunities simultaneously.

Corner advantage: Corners are the second-best positions after center.
