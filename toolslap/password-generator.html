Password Security Tips

  • Use passwords that are at least 12 characters long
  • Include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid using personal information like names, birthdays, or addresses
  • Use a unique password for each of your accounts
  • Consider using a password manager to store your passwords securely
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available
  • Change passwords immediately if you suspect they've been compromised
  • Never share your passwords via email or text messages
