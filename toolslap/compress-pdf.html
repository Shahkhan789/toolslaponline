Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I compress my PDF? +
Compression rates vary depending on the PDF content. Text-heavy PDFs can typically be compressed by 50-70%, while image-heavy PDFs may achieve 80-90% size reduction. Our smart compression analyzes your document to achieve the best possible reduction while maintaining quality.
Will compressing affect PDF quality? +
Our intelligent compression algorithm is designed to maintain visual quality while reducing file size. The "Balanced" setting provides excellent compression with minimal quality loss, while "Minimal" prioritizes quality preservation. You can choose the level that best suits your needs.
Is there a file size limit for compression? +
There are no artificial file size limits imposed by our tool. The only limitation is your browser's memory capacity, which typically handles files up to several hundred MB without issues. For very large files, processing may take longer but will still work.
What compression techniques are used? +
We use multiple compression techniques including image optimization, font subsetting, content stream compression, and duplicate object removal. The algorithm automatically selects the best combination of techniques based on your PDF's content structure.
