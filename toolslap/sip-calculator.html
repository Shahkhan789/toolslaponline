What is SIP?

  • SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is a disciplined way of investing in mutual funds
  • It allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly (monthly/quarterly) in mutual funds
  • SIP helps in averaging the cost of investment and reduces market volatility impact
  • It leverages the power of compounding to grow your wealth over time
  • You can start SIP with as low as ₹100 per month in most mutual funds
  • SIP investments are flexible - you can increase, decrease, or stop anytime
  • Tax-saving mutual funds (ELSS) offer additional benefits under Section 80C
