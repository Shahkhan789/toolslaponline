Voice Licensing Guide

Understanding commercial rights and usage terms for AI-generated voices

Commercial License

Full Rights
  • Use in commercial projects
  • Resell generated content
  • Client work & agency use
  • Broadcast & streaming
  • Unlimited distribution

Examples: ElevenLabs, Murf AI, Synthesys, Azure Speech

Personal License

Limited Rights
  • Personal projects only
  • Educational content
  • No commercial use
  • No resale allowed
  • Limited distribution

Examples: Some free tiers, open-source TTS models

Attribution Required

Restricted
  • Must credit provider
  • Link to original service
  • Usage limits apply
  • Cannot modify branding
  • Platform restrictions

Examples: Some open-source tools, research models

Licensing Best Practices

Before Using:

  • • Read terms of service carefully
  • • Check commercial usage rights
  • • Verify attribution requirements
  • • Understand usage limits

For Commercial Projects:

  • • Choose licensed commercial tools
  • • Keep licensing documentation
  • • Consider voice ownership rights
  • • Check geographic restrictions
